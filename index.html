Your Questions, Answered.

Read more
What is ecocide?–
Ecocide is the widespread, severe, or long-term destruction of ecosystems, often for economic gain, and frequently carried out by trafficked and forced labor. Slavery and environmental destruction are two faces of the same extractive system.
What is human trafficking?+
Human trafficking is the use of force, fraud, or coercion to exploit people for labor or commercial sex. It rarely looks the way films depict it, traffickers prey on vulnerability, debt, and displacement more often than physical restraint.
What is social innovation?+
Social innovation means building sustainable enterprises and tools that prevent trafficking before it starts, creating economic alternatives so exploitation loses its grip. It's the engine behind REBBL, Dignita, and Regenerate.
